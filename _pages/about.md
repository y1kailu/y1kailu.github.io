Thanks for stopping by! I am a third-year undergraduate student at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, majoring in Data Science and minoring in Mathematics. My research interests focus on reinforcement learning theory and control theory, particularly their intersections with world models and agentic AI. As I continue my studies, I hope to further explore broader mathematical methods and their applications across different fields.

Currently, I am very fortunate to be advised by Tongxin Li at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, and co-advised by Adam Wierman at Caltech CMS. I am deeply grateful to all my advisors and collaborators for their guidance and support.
